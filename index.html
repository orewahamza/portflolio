Hey, I'm Hamza — a full-stack web developer specializing in building modern web applications with the MERN stack.

I got into web development because I love solving problems and turning ideas into real, working products that people actually use. Whether it's a learning platform with complex quiz systems or an e-commerce store, I focus on building solutions that work.

What I Build:

  • • Interactive platforms with admin dashboards
  • • E-commerce solutions with shopping carts & order management
  • • SaaS applications with user authentication & role-based access
  • • RESTful APIs and database architecture
  • • Responsive, mobile-first interfaces that look great on any device

How I Work:

I believe in clear communication and realistic timelines. When you hire me, you get regular updates, clean documented code, and a product that works exactly as promised. No surprises, no excuses — just solid work delivered on time.

SERVICES

Here's what I can build for you

Full Website — Front & Back

I can build you a complete website from scratch — the part users see (the design) and the part that runs behind the scenes (the server). Using React, Node.js, MongoDB and more, your site will look great and work reliably.

  • Full website built from scratch
  • Works on all screen sizes
  • Login, data storage & more — all connected

Frontend-Only or Backend-Only

Need just the visual side of your site, or just the server logic? I can do either. That said, I strongly recommend building both together — when one person builds everything, the two sides connect perfectly and you avoid a lot of headaches.

  • Frontend (UI) only — available
  • Backend (server/API) only — available
  • Both together — recommended

Hosting & Going Live

Once your project is ready, I can help you put it online. You just need to have a server and a domain name — I'll take care of all the technical setup to get your site live and running smoothly.

  • Deploy your site to your server
  • Connect your custom domain
  • You provide the server & domain, I do the rest
